Points To Remember

You may need to define a custom authentication entry point when

  1. You may want to do some pre-authentication work like count number of attempts.
  2. You may want to authenticate from an external source e.g third party authentication.
or for any other reason. In such cases you will need to define your own Authentication Entry point.
